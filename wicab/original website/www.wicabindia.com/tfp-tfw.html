Wicab TFP

Features

  • Composite Flouropolymers and Fluorocarbons insulated.
  • Copper conductor (solid).
  • Conductor diameter size from 1.40 mm to 3.00 mm.
  • Operating temperature from - 500C upto +1400C.
  • BDV >3KV, low leakage current.
  • Operating frequency 50Hz to 60 Hz (± 5Hz).
  • Exceptional mechanical protection.
  • Current density application 15-20 amp per sq mm
  • Suitable for Industrial applications, chemical processing plants, oil extraction and in varied environmental conditions.
Wicab TFW
  • Composite Fluoropolymers and fluorocarbons insulated.
  • Copper conductor (solid), Conductor diameter size from 1.10 mm to 3.00 mm.
  • Operating temperature from - 2000C upto +3200C.
  • Inert to all known chemicals, suitable for under abnormal environmental conditions (thermal variations, voltage variations, chemical inertness).
  • BDV >5KV, low leakage current.
  • Operating frequency 50Hz to 60 Hz (((±10Hz).
  • Current density application upto 15-20 amp per sq mm, suitable for application with high voltage variations, long duration high voltage spikes and transient voltage conditions.
  • Suitable for any kind of Industrial application, insulated wire inert to all known chemicals and gases and static to thermal variation.
