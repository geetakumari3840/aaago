Bare Wire Products
Wicab Engineering P Ltd manufacture and supply following different Bare wire products in various sizes:

1. Bare copper wire (Solid):

  • Annealed, Bare Copper, Soft drawn solid conductor Circular cross section; Size from 0.05 mm to 3.40mm (AWG 46 to AWG 6) as per BS or ASTM or BIS/IS standards;

2. Bare copper wire (Stranded / Bunched):

  • Plain, Stranded, Bunched, single-core;
  • Compacted /non-compacted,
  • Circular Shaped copper conductor; Sizes with nominal cross sectional Area from as small as 0.10 sq mm and up to 600 sq mm;

3. Metal coated / Metal Plated Copper conductor:

  • Solid or stranded / bunched
  • Copper wire coated /plated with either Silver or Nickel or Tin;As per BS or ASTM or US-MIL standards;

4. Silver Plated Nickel; Silver plated / Coated Nickel wire;

  • Solid or stranded / bunched;
  • Size up to AWG 42;

5. Nickel wire and Nickel Alloys:

  • Bare Nickel solid wire; NiCr Alloy Solid wire;
  • Size from AWG 16 and up to AWG 38;

6. Resistance wires / resistance Alloys – as per the requirement up to AWG 40;

7. Copper Clad Steel (CCS):

  • as per BS 4087 grade 2 Standards; conductivity of 30% or 40% as per requirement; Sizes (AWG 32 to AWG 16);

8. Copper Clad Aluminum (CCA): Plant under installation and development;

9. Stranded / Bunched Copper wire –

  • As per ASTM Standards or BS standards; BS 6360 class 1, class 2, class 5 or class 6; as per required / defined
  • Shape and lay; flexible; single core and multi-core cables;
