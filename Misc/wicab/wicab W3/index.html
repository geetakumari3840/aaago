     Company Profile

        WICAB Engineering Private Ltd. an ISO 9001:2000 Company is established in the industrial area of NCR New Delhi, India. WICAB is a pioneer in designing and manufacturing of special wires and cables for electrical and electronic products for various industrial segments under the brand ‘Wicab’, with electrical insulation base of PTFE (Teflon), Silicon Rubber and Polyester/ Polymers, Plastics, Kapton, Mylar, FHP, FHF etc. Wicab’s specialisation is in designing high performance special wires and cables with fine insulations, fine size and accuracy & weight, with excellent Electrical & Electronic characteristics & properties in capacitive, inductive and resistive loads under various mechanical, thermal, metallurgical & chemical environmental conditions suitable for customized & industrial applications and equipments for the niche OEMs in domestic and overseas..

       Wicab has got well-defined development and manufacturing process to ensure products of high quality and consistency. We are manufacturing the products as per UL, CSA, BS, US-MIL, IEC and BIS/IS standards and following the process for getting CE marking for our products. Wicab has got the large corporate and Industrial organizations of repute, in India and overseas, as its customers.

     Services

        WICAB Engineering Private Ltd. an ISO 9001:2000 A Technology Driven organisation provides various services:

       Wicab has expertise in insulating material & Insulation process for different conductors. Design aspects based upon: working temperature (–500C to +6000C); Working voltage - upto 50 kV / BDV upto 90kV, low leakage current (a few mA) High conductor current density (upto 50 Amp/sqmm); Conductor to Insulation Area Ratio as high as 67:33: excellent mechanical parameters and Insulation Inertness for various known chemicals.

  1. Professional Advisory Services - for application of wires and cables in various industrial and process industries and also for Appliances and equipments.

  2. Design services - For providing the design and development of wires and cables based upon the application parameters and Usage environment like Marine, Mines and collieries, Chemical plants considering the various Electrical, Mechanical, Thermal parameters as well as Chemical Environmental conditions while considering the required Physical Dimensions.

  3. Repairs & maintenance services -For providing repairs and maintenance services for costly cables.

  4. Testing and Measurment services - We provide the testing services on different Wires and cables for different parameters through our test laboratory.
